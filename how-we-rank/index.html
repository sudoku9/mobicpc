Ranking Criteria

Each carrier is evaluated across five weighted categories. Our scoring system ensures that the most important factors for consumers have the greatest impact on rankings.

Value & Pricing
30% Weight

We evaluate the cost-effectiveness of plans, hidden fees, promotional pricing, and overall value for money.

Key Factors:

  • Monthly plan costs and fees
  • Promotional pricing accuracy
  • Hidden charges and contract terms
  • Price per GB of data
  • Family plan discounts
Network Coverage
25% Weight

Network performance and coverage area are crucial for user experience. We test real-world performance across different locations.

Key Factors:

  • Urban and rural coverage maps
  • 5G availability and speeds
  • Data speed consistency
  • Network reliability
  • Roaming capabilities
Customer Service
20% Weight

Quality of customer support, response times, and problem resolution capabilities significantly impact user satisfaction.

Key Factors:

  • Support channel availability
  • Response time accuracy
  • Problem resolution rates
  • Customer satisfaction scores
  • Self-service options
Features & Benefits
15% Weight

Additional features, perks, and plan flexibility that enhance the overall mobile experience.

Key Factors:

  • International calling options
  • Mobile hotspot data
  • Streaming service perks
  • Plan customization options
  • Device upgrade programs
Reliability & Trust
10% Weight

Company reputation, financial stability, and long-term reliability for customers.

Key Factors:

  • Company financial stability
  • Industry reputation
  • User review aggregation
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Transparency in practices

Our Research Process

We follow a rigorous four-step process to ensure our rankings are accurate, current, and reflect real-world user experiences.

1

Data Collection

We gather comprehensive data from carriers including pricing, coverage maps, feature lists, and official specifications. This includes both current and historical data to identify trends.

2

Real-World Testing

Our team conducts hands-on testing of network performance, customer service interactions, and actual user experiences across multiple locations and scenarios.

3

User Feedback Analysis

We analyze thousands of user reviews, satisfaction surveys, and feedback from various sources to understand real customer experiences and common issues.

4

Weighted Scoring

Each carrier receives a score based on our weighted criteria. Scores are calculated using standardized metrics and are regularly updated to reflect current market conditions.

Transparency & Independence

Editorial Independence

Our rankings are never influenced by advertising relationships or carrier partnerships. We maintain strict editorial independence to ensure unbiased recommendations.

Open Methodology

We openly share our ranking methodology and regularly update it based on industry changes and user feedback. Transparency builds trust.
